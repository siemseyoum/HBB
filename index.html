Introduction

This website is all about influencing the young generation to stay active and healthy. The content covers all type of mental and physical benefits you can achieve from basketball. As you explore the website further you will find more information covered in different method allowing you to see the whole picture. I have covered an audio, video and six photography that all explains what basketball is all about.

The audio contains interviews with active teenagers who have already taken part in this activity. I have interviewed two people from different gender to get the idea across. The interviews contain questions the reader is mostly likely to ask. The question is designed to help the reader understand why exercising daily can be beneficial for their mental, physical and social life.

This documentary is dedicated to the health benefit of basketball. There are many reason to why a young adult should stay healthy through exercise. The video documentary explores the idea of how basketball can make beneficial impact in someone’s life. There is a detailed explanation on what type of change should someone expect when applying this to their lifestyle. It is a slow paced video making it easy to read and understand the information.

Basketball is one of the most famous sport across the world. The past paced game involves jumping and running which the essential to maintain a healthy body as well as preventing chronic diseases. Traditionally, endurance and strength based activity have been suggested for improving health profile.

Team sport activities are associated with positive social experience which may be crucial for motivation young generation to stay outdoor longer getting their daily exercise. Basketball also benefits the mental cognition while enhancing your motor skills, coordination, boost self-image and improves sleeping habits and reduces stress and anxiety.

Basketball is suitable for all ages, there is no doubt that excising daily can make a big impact to the human body, playing basketball has many health benefit whether you are playing for fun or looking for a lifestyle change. Turning basketball into a life style is best way to develop new friends as well to create a team environment. Meeting new people from different background and social group who share the same interest has never been easier. If you are looking to improve your physical and mental aspect while having fun and socialising, basketball is capable of transforming your life for the better.
